<p><strong>The Hemoperfusion Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Activated Carbon Adsorption Hemoperfusion Device</li><li>Resin Adsorption Hemoperfusion Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hemoperfusion device market is primarily segmented into two types: activated carbon adsorption devices and resin adsorption devices. Activated carbon devices utilize porous carbon to adsorb toxins and waste products from the blood effectively, making them suitable for treating various poisoning and overdose cases. In contrast, resin adsorption devices employ specific ion-exchange resins to selectively remove certain substances, like bile acids and uremic toxins, offering targeted treatment options. Both types enhance detoxification and improve patient outcomes in critical care settings.</p></p>
