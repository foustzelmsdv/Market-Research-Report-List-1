<p><strong>The Dental Fiber Post Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fiberglass Posts</li><li>Carbon Fiber Posts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental fiber post market is primarily divided into two types: fiberglass posts and carbon fiber posts. Fiberglass posts are known for their excellent esthetics and biocompatibility, offering a natural color that blends well with dentin. They provide good bonding with resin materials and flexibility, reducing the risk of tooth fracture. On the other hand, carbon fiber posts are recognized for their high strength and rigidity, making them suitable for load-bearing applications. They absorb stress effectively but may have limited esthetic appeal. Each type serves distinct clinical needs.</p></p>
