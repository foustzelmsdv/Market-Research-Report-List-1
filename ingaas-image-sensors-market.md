<p><strong>The InGaAs Image Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>InGaAs Linear Image Sensors</li><li>InGaAs Area Image Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The InGaAs image sensors market is primarily categorized into two types: InGaAs linear image sensors and InGaAs area image sensors. InGaAs linear image sensors are designed to capture images in a one-dimensional format, making them ideal for applications like spectroscopy and barcode scanning, where fast scanning of extended objects is needed. In contrast, InGaAs area image sensors capture two-dimensional images, suited for applications such as thermal imaging and low-light photography, providing comprehensive scene analysis and improved detection capabilities.</p></p>
