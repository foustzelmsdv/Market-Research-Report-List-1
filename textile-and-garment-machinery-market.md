<p><p>The textile and garment machinery market comprises various segments that cater to different stages of fabric production. Spinning machinery transforms raw fibers into yarn, essential for textiles. Knitting machinery produces fabric by interlocking yarns, offering versatility in design. Weaving machinery creates woven fabrics through the interlacing of yarns, vital for various textile products. Finishing machinery enhances fabric characteristics through processes like dyeing and coating, ensuring quality and durability. Together, these segments support the comprehensive textile manufacturing process.</p></p>
