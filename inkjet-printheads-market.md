<p><p>The inkjet printhead market comprises three main types: piezoelectric printheads, thermal printheads, and others. Piezoelectric printheads utilize electrical voltage to control ink ejection, offering precision for high-quality images. Thermal printheads, on the other hand, generate heat to vaporize ink, providing fast printing suitable for everyday consumer applications. "Others" include varied technologies like continuous inkjet and solvent ink systems, catering to specialized needs. Each type serves distinct market segments, from professional printing to home use, enhancing versatility and performance.</p></p>
