<p><strong>The Carburetor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm Carburetor</li><li>Float-Feed Carburetor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carburetor market is primarily divided into two types: diaphragm carburetors and float-feed carburetors. Diaphragm carburetors utilize a flexible diaphragm that responds to changes in airflow, adjusting fuel delivery for optimal performance, often found in smaller engines like those in lawn mowers and recreational vehicles. In contrast, float-feed carburetors maintain a constant fuel level using a float mechanism, providing consistent fuel supply, commonly used in automotive and larger engine applications. Each type serves specific needs based on engine requirements and operational efficiency.</p></p>
