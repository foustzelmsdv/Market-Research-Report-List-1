<p><p>Pipe extrusion lines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their design and operational orientation. Vertical type lines utilize a vertical setup that allows gravity to assist in material flow, often leading to compact installations suitable for smaller spaces. In contrast, horizontal type lines feature a horizontal orientation, which is ideal for larger production capacities and provides easier access for maintenance. Both designs cater to specific production needs, influencing efficiency, space utilization, and ease of operation.</p></p>
