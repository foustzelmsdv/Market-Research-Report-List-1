<p><ul><li>Direct Drying</li><li>Indirect Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial flash dryers market is divided into two primary types: direct drying and indirect drying. Direct drying involves the rapid evaporation of moisture from materials by exposing them to hot air or gas streams, resulting in quick processing times and high efficiency. Indirect drying, on the other hand, utilizes heated surfaces or indirect heat transfer methods to evaporate moisture without direct contact with the drying medium, offering better temperature control and preserving temperature-sensitive materials. Both methods serve distinct applications in various industries.</p></p>
