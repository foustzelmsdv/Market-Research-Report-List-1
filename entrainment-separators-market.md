<p><ul><li>Wire-mesh Entrainment Separators</li><li>Baffle-type Entrainment Separators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Entrainment separators are crucial for removing liquid droplets from gas streams in various industrial applications. The wire-mesh entrainment separator utilizes a mesh structure that captures and coalesces droplets, promoting efficient separation. In contrast, baffle-type entrainment separators use a series of baffles to redirect and slow down the flow, facilitating droplet removal through gravity. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing system performance, reducing operational issues, and ensuring compliance with environmental standards by minimizing emissions from industrial processes.</p></p>
