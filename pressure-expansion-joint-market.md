<p><p>The Pressure Expansion Joint market is primarily categorized into two main types: Two-Way Pressure Expansion Joints and Three-Way Pressure Expansion Joints. Two-Way Pressure Expansion Joints allow movement in two directions, accommodating thermal expansion and contraction in piping systems. In contrast, Three-Way Pressure Expansion Joints provide additional flexibility by allowing movement in three directions, effectively managing complex piping layouts and preventing stress on the system. Both types enhance system durability and performance by mitigating the effects of thermal fluctuations.</p></p>
