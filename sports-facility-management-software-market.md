<p><p>Sports facility management software can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises software is installed directly on a facility's local servers, offering control and security but requiring maintenance and updates. Conversely, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, providing easier access, scalability, and flexibility with lower upfront costs. Users can access these solutions from anywhere with an internet connection, making cloud options increasingly popular for managing sports facilities effectively and efficiently.</p></p>
