<p><p>The plasma nitriding furnace market is categorized into three main types based on operating temperatures. Low temperature furnaces operate typically below 400°C, offering minimal distortion and maintaining the integrity of complex geometries. Medium temperature furnaces, ranging from 400°C to 600°C, provide a balance between diffusion and hardness. High temperature furnaces, exceeding 600°C, achieve deeper nitrogen penetration and enhanced hardness but may risk greater material distortion. Each type serves distinct applications in surface hardening and improving wear resistance of metal components.</p></p>
