<p><strong>The Cover Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat Activated Cover Tapes</li><li>Pressure-sensitive Cover Tapes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cover Tapes market is primarily categorized into two types: heat-activated and pressure-sensitive cover tapes. Heat-activated cover tapes bond when exposed to heat, making them ideal for applications requiring strong adhesion and durability. In contrast, pressure-sensitive cover tapes adhere upon applying pressure, offering versatility and ease of use without needing heat. Both types are essential in various industries, including electronics, packaging, and medical, where reliable sealing and protection of surfaces are crucial in manufacturing and assembly processes.</p></p>
