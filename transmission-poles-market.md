<p><p>The transmission poles market comprises four main types: wood, concrete, steel, and fiberglass poles. Concrete poles offer enhanced durability and resistance to harsh weather but are heavier and more expensive. Steel poles provide strength and longevity, making them suitable for heavy-load applications, while fiberglass poles are lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and ideal for challenging environments. Each type has unique advantages catering to specific infrastructure needs.</p></p>
