<p><strong>The Home Dialysis Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hemodialysis (HD)</li><li>Peritoneal dialysis (PD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home dialysis systems primarily include two types: Hemodialysis (HD) and Peritoneal Dialysis (PD). Hemodialysis involves using a machine to filter waste from the blood, typically requiring sessions several times a week. It offers the advantage of efficient waste removal but may demand more technical expertise for setup. In contrast, Peritoneal Dialysis utilizes the lining of the abdomen to filter blood, allowing for greater flexibility and independence, as it can often be performed overnight or during the day.</p></p>
