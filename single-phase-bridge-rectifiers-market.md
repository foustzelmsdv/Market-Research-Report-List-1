<p><p>The single-phase bridge rectifier market is categorized into several types based on mounting techniques. SMD (Surface Mount Device) and SMT (Surface Mount Technology) rectifiers are designed for compact assembly on circuit boards, facilitating automated manufacturing processes. Screw mount rectifiers are typically utilized in applications requiring secure connections and easier installation. Through hole rectifiers offer robust mounting on PCBs, appealing to traditional designs and providing better thermal management. Each type serves distinct application needs, balancing size, stability, and ease of use.</p></p>
