<p><p>The Glass Blood Collection Tubes Market primarily serves two key applications: venous blood collection and capillary blood collection. Both applications leverage glass tubes' advantages, such as chemical resistance and sterility, ensuring reliable results in clinical and laboratory settings.</p></p>
