<p><p>The uterine fibroid treatment market consists of various techniques aimed at alleviating symptoms and addressing fibroid growth. Endometrial ablation removes the uterine lining to reduce bleeding. MRI-guided procedures allow precise targeting of fibroids using imaging technology. Hysterectomy involves the surgical removal of the uterus, while myomectomy focuses on removing only the fibroids. Uterine artery embolization blocks blood flow to fibroids, shrinking them. Radiofrequency ablation uses heat to destroy fibroid tissue. Other treatments may include medication and alternative therapies.</p></p>
