<p><p>The carbide blank market is primarily categorized into two types: coarse grain tungsten carbide (WC) and fine grain tungsten carbide (WC). Coarse grain WC is known for its toughness and resistance to wear, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications like mining and drilling tools. The choice between these types depends on the specific application's requirements for durability and performance.</p></p>
