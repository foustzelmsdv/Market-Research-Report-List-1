<p><p>A microstrip antenna, also known as a patch antenna, is a type of radio antenna fabricated using printed circuit techniques. It consists of a thin conductive patch on a dielectric substrate, with a ground plane on the opposite side. These antennas are favored for their lightweight, low-profile design, and ease of integration with other circuit components, making them ideal for applications in mobile devices, satellite communications, and wireless systems.</p><p>The Microstrip Antenna Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by the increasing demand for communication devices, IoT applications, and advanced wireless technologies. Key sectors, including aerospace and defense, automotive, and consumer electronics, are embracing microstrip antennas to achieve enhanced performance and miniaturization.
