<p><p>Linear actuators for industrial machinery are crucial for precise motion control. The market can be segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Pneumatic actuators** use compressed air to produce linear movement, offering rapid actuation and lightweight design, ideal for applications needing quick, repetitive motion.</p><p>2. **Electric actuators** provide accurate positioning and control through electric motors, favored for their energy efficiency and low maintenance.</p><p>3. **Hydraulic actuators** utilize hydraulic fluid pressure for powerful linear motion, suitable for heavy-duty applications requiring significant force and robustness.</p></p>
