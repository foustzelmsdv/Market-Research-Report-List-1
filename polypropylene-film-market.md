<p><strong>The Polypropylene Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cast Polypropylene (CPP) Film</li><li>Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) film</li><li>Inflated polypropylene (IPP) Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypropylene film market includes three main types: Cast Polypropylene (CPP) film, known for its clarity and flexibility, making it suitable for packaging applications; Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) film, which is stretched in two directions, offering enhanced strength and barrier properties, ideal for food packaging and labels; and Inflated Polypropylene (IPP) film, produced by inflating polypropylene resin, providing a lightweight and cost-effective solution for various applications, including bags and wraps. Each type serves distinct industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
