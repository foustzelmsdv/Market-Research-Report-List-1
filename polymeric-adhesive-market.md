<p><strong>The Polymeric Adhesive Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Adhesive</li><li>Thermoplastic Adhesive</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymeric adhesive market comprises three main types: thermosetting adhesives, thermoplastic adhesives, and others. Thermosetting adhesives cure to form a permanent bond when heat or chemicals are applied, making them suitable for high-temperature applications. Thermoplastic adhesives can be remelted and reshaped, offering flexibility and ease of use for various applications. The "others" category includes specialized adhesives with unique properties tailored for specific industries, such as UV-cured or pressure-sensitive adhesives, catering to diverse bonding needs across sectors.</p></p>
