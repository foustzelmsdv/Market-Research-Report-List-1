<p><p>The 5-axis micromachining system market encompasses several types of machining processes. Electro Discharge Machining (EDM) utilizes electrical discharges to shape materials with high precision. Electrochemical Machining (ECM) employs electrolysis to remove material, ideal for intricate designs. Laser Machining uses focused laser beams for precision cutting and engraving, suitable for various materials. Traditional Machining includes classic methods like milling and turning, but lacks the precision of newer technologies. Each type offers unique advantages for micro-scale applications in various industries.</p></p>
