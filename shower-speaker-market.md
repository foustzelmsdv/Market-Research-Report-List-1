<p><ul><li>Hanging</li><li>Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shower speaker market can be categorized into two main types: hanging and adsorption. Hanging shower speakers feature hooks or straps that allow them to be suspended from a showerhead or wall, providing easy access and positioning for optimal sound. In contrast, adsorption shower speakers rely on suction cups to attach securely to smooth surfaces, ensuring stability and convenience during use. Both types cater to consumers seeking waterproof audio solutions for enhancing their shower experiences.</p></p>
