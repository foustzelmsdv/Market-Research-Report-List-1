<p><p>The telecentric camera objective market features distinct types, including object square telephoto lenses and bi-telecentric lenses. Object square telephoto lenses are designed to maintain consistent magnification across the field, minimizing perspective distortion, making them ideal for imaging applications requiring accurate measurements. Bi-telecentric lenses, on the other hand, allow for both object-side and image-side telecentricity, ensuring that light rays remain parallel at the entrance and exit, which enhances measurement precision in industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
