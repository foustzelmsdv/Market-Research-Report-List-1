<p><ul><li>Termite Spray</li><li>Termite Powder</li><li>Termite Baits</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The termite control products market is divided into three main types: termite sprays, termite powders, and termite baits. Termite sprays deliver liquid insecticides that offer immediate protection by targeting existing infestations. Termite powders are granular formulations that can be applied to affected areas, disrupting the termite life cycle. Termite baits consist of strategically placed stations containing slow-acting toxic substances that attract termites, allowing for targeted elimination over time. Each type addresses different aspects of termite management and prevention.</p></p>
