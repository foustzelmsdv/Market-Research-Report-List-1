<p><ul><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearing</li><li>Four Point Contact Bearings</li><li>Angular Contact Bearing</li><li>Crossed Roller Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The humanoid robot bearings market is segmented into various types essential for optimizing robotic movement and performance. Deep groove ball bearings offer low friction and handle radial and axial loads, making them ideal for joints. Four point contact bearings provide high load capacity and precision in applications requiring compact designs. Angular contact bearings are designed for high-speed operations, supporting axial loads. Crossed roller bearings excel in applications demanding high stiffness and rotational accuracy, crucial for humanoid robotic structures and articulation.</p></p>
